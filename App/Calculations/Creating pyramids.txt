variables:

Number of sides (n)
Radius (r)
Height of the pyramid's apex (h)
Slant height (s)
Side width (w)
Leg rotation angle (α)
Leg length (L)
Inward tilt angle (φ)

1. Base: θ = 360° / n 
w = 2 x r x tan (180°/n) 

2. Constructing triangles for pyramid
Width of the triangle is already determined by the pyramid's side width. 

Slant Height: $$s = \sqrt{r^2 + h^2}$$
Leg Length: $$L = \sqrt{s^2 + (w/2)^2}$$ 
Leg Rotation angle: $$\alpha = \arctan\left(\frac{s}{w/2}\right)$$ 

3. Inward rotation: $$\phi = \arctan(h / r)$$ then 90 - φ
