Variables:
Rotation angle (θ)
Side Width (w)
Radius (r)
Number of sides (n)

Calculation:

Rotation angle (θ)

θ = 360 / n

Side width (w)

w = 2 x r x tan (180° / n)

Example:

Let's say I want to create a 24-sided polygon.

1. Calculate the rotation angle

θ = 360° / 24 = 15
The rotation angle for each side is 15 units

2. Let's pick the radius to be 15

w = 2 x 15 x tan (180° / 24)
w = 30 x tan (7.5°)
w = 30 x 0.13165
w ≈ 3.9495 studs
